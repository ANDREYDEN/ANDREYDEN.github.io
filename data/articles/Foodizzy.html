Problem

  1. Households produce too much food waste because of lack of food management.
  2. Planning meals ahead while keeping track of ingredients is notoriously hard.

Solution

Foodizzy is a mobile app that combines a simple shopping list with a food storage system which allows users to quickly check what they already have in their fridge when searching for new ingredients. Users can add new items to their shopping/storage lists, move them around or mark them as consumed/wasted. The app also tracks statistics about food consumtion to give insight into different kinds of foods being used and/or thrown away.
