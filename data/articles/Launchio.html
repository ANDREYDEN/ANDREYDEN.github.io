Background

This was my first ever hackathon experience and one of the most memorable ones. Space Apps is a NASA and CSA sponsored annual hackathon held all over the globe.

My team and I had around 20 hours to develop an application that would show past and future rocket launches in different locations around the world. We decided to categorize the launches by place and time and show them on an interactive map with a 'cool' design.

Technical Details

For this challenge we decided to use Python's Dash library for web data visualization. We didn't know the tool at the time so it was quite a challenge to learn it, as the library was pretty fresh lacking documentation and active community. Now it is one of the go-to's for data visualization with Python
