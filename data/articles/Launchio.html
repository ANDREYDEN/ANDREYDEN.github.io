Launchio

October 2018

This was my first ever hackathon experience and one of the most memorable ones.

My team and I had 20ish hours to develop an application that would show past and future rocket launches in different locations around the world. We decided to categorize the launches by place and time and show them on an interactive map with a 'cool' design.

For this challenge we decided to use Python's Dash library for web data visualization. We didn't know the tool at the time so it was quite a challenge to learn it, as the library was pretty fresh lacking documentation and active community.
