Background

This was my first ever project made with Unity. I was doing contractor/freelancing work at the time and needed to learn Unity to develop a mobile application for Maple Mana. So I decided to make a simple platformer where you would control the character by changing the gravity.

The Game

You are an astronaut on a mission: collect valuable crystals and return to your spaceship. The only thing you were provided with is the gravitex - a special device that allows you to change gravity. Jump between platforms, collect crystals and watch out for the void...
